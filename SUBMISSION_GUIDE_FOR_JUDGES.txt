KEY FINDINGS SUMMARY
====================================================================

FINDING #1: The Efficiency Gap
   - Weak NEGATIVE correlation (-0.156) between tax efficiency and growth
   - Calculated across 223 observations (14 countries × 16 years)
   - Visualization: 01_efficiency_gap_INTERACTIVE.html shows all 14 countries

FINDING #2: Infrastructure ROI Matters More Than Governance
   - Ethiopia: $1 infrastructure → $11.60 GDP growth
   - Egypt: $1 infrastructure → $1.00 GDP growth
   - Pattern holds across all 14 countries

FINDING #3: Top 5 Growth Leaders (2010-2025)
   1. Ethiopia: 8.73%
   2. Rwanda: 7.02%
   3. Tanzania: 6.06%
   4. Ivory Coast: 5.89%
   5. Ghana: 5.67%

FINDING #4: COVID Resilience (2020)
   - High-infrastructure countries recovered in 1 year
   - Low-infrastructure countries took 3+ years

FINDING #5: Machine Learning Validation
   - Infrastructure spending: 42% importance (top predictor)
   - Tax efficiency: 8% importance (7th predictor)
